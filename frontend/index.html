About Pradeep AI

Pradeep AI is a premium, multi-talented AI assistant designed for seamless communication and creativity.

Core Features:

  • Voice Chat AI: Talk to the bot in multiple languages including Tamil. Use "Auto-Speak" for a hands-free experience.
  • Instant Image Generation: Ask the bot to "generate an image" directly in chat or use the Image Studio for high-quality visuals.
  • Multi-language Support: Fully localized UI and AI processing for Tamil, Spanish, French, and German.
  • Private & Secure: Local authentication system to keep your conversations private.

Built With: Node.js, Express, Gemini API, and Pollinations AI.
